Company Overview

NDU VIK GAS LTD is a Nigerian energy and gas distribution company established to provide safe, reliable, and affordable Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) and related energy solutions to households, businesses, institutions, and industries. The company is committed to promoting cleaner cooking energy through the supply of quality cooking gas, gas cylinders, regulators, hoses, gas cookers, and related accessories while maintaining the highest standards of safety, customer service, and operational excellence.
